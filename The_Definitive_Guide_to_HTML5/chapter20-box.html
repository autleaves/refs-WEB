There are lots of different kinks of fruit - there are over 500 varieties of banana alone. By the time we add the countless types of apples, orange, and other well-know fruit, we are faced with thousand of choices.

One of the most interesting aspects of fruit is the varity available in each country. I live near London, in an area which is known for its apples. When travelling in Asia, I was struck by how many different kinds of banana were available - many of which had unique flavours and which were only available within a small region.
